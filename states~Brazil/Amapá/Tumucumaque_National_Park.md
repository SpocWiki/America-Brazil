## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Tumucumaque Mountains National Park (Portuguese: 
> Parque Nacional Montanhas do Tumucumaque; Portuguese pronunciation: [tumukuˈmaki]) 
> is situated  in the Amazon rainforest in the Brazilian states of Amapá and Pará. 
> It is bordered to the north by French Guiana and Suriname.
